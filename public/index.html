We need more water polo players

I guess we don’t need them, but I really want more players. I hate playing with college kids who are going to quit next semester. I’m tired of seeing the same dudes every summer at the same shrinking tournament. I want more women. I want more LGBTQ people. I want more moms and dads. I want more Black players, Asian players, Latin American players. I even, God help me, want the Australians.

I’ll guard the new guy so you don’t have to, and yes he’s kind of lost sometimes, but he’s not that bad. I’ll take that lady on my team even though she wears rec-specs. If she’s so terrible, why didn’t you block her lob? Stop saying “if we just took this seriously, we could be, like really good.” If we took this any more seriously we’d have no one left.

Enough with the Olympics. I love the Olympics, but it’s a long wait. I mean every 4 years.. Come on! Let’s play right now, watch some good polo right now, do something right now.

Stop telling everyone how hard the sport is! I want absolutely zero more conversations that go, “Yes you have to tread water the whole time. Yes, stuff happens under the water. One time this guy punched me in the-” No one’s impressed. No one cares. Why are we making it so hard to do cool stuff? The shot clock is too short. The pool is too long. There’s nowhere to drive. Do picks ever work? Does that guy ever get back on defense? USC vs UCLA is usually a good game, but I don’t want my whole sport to just be a feeder for a few interesting games a year.

Solutions:

  • Modified rules for smaller pools/fields of play
  • Youtube friendly content created in a youtube style
  • Alternative rules for people who don’t swim 4000 meters every morning
  • Consciously adopting a cultural that invites more fans and athletes
